About Me

Name: Juan Manuel Taborda Velásquez

Grade: 9th C

Age: 15 Years Old

My artistic research focuses on exploring visual plasticity and relief through a meticulous methodology, where analytical engineering principles converge with the boldness of contemporary design.

Furthermore, this proposal merges the dynamic, high-contrast nature of pop art with the intricate patterns of abstract pointillism. By applying extreme color saturation and a deliberate "horror vacui" technique, I transform ordinary objects and natural landscapes into tangible, physical structures that pulsate with calculating rhythm and strict geometric harmony.
